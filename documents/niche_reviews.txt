Socially, there were frat parties and on campus houses parties, dozens of clubs to 
join, and the college hosted events. Meadville itself wasn't too big, but Erie was only
30 minutes away. Peak N Peak skiing as a gym class was fun. Greek life exists but isn't
overwhelming, and people are friends regardless of whether they were Greek.
