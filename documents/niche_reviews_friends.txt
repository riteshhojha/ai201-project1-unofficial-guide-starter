I spent First Semester out of High School at Allegheny and then left to a larger university where I am much happier
. I thought a small school was for me, but quickly found it boring and difficult to make friends. If you are not
in a sport or group already, I found it difficult...Academically challenging but that was expected. I made more
friends at new university in 2 days then I did the whole semester at Allegheny....Student just seem a "little off"
or not really interested in making new friends from my perspective...Covid did not help the situation of course...
I made several visits and stayed overnight before accepting and did not think I would have social problems at all
