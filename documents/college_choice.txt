College Choice Reviews (collegechoice.net)

Source: www.collegechoice.net
Type: Educational directory and ranking site
Description: Helps students simplify college options through rankings and comparisons

Features:
- Ranking categories for best online colleges, affordable programs, specialized degrees
- Navigation by location (state-based college searches)
- Degree filtering (associate, bachelor's, master's, doctoral programs)
- Topic guides for admissions, financial aid, scholarships
- Popular rankings across business, healthcare, engineering fields

Key Insight: Focuses on making college selection more accessible through specialized ranking categories and state-based navigation.
