Vibe: middle of the road politically. “Study like an ivy, party like a state school” 
- though that may be an exaggeration. Pretty traditional NE liberal arts college.
Greek life present, but only 25-30%. Student body not too ethnically diverse. 
More economically diverse, however, than lots of top-tier liberal arts schools. 
Students from PA, OH, NY, NJ

Excellent write up in the book, “Colleges that Change Lives”.
