Over 130 clubs and organizations give Gators the opportunity to participate in a
wide range of activities. You might gain experience with organizing, facilitating,
marketing, finances, and leadership, in addition to building friendships and just
having fun! Allegheny clubs and organizations are open to all students without
respect to sex, race, creed, national origin, sexual orientation, or
ability/disability, and it’s our students who lead the way in establishing
and running the organizations they care about most — what groups will you
be a part of?
